Speaker Details

Patrick Ventuzelo

Patrick Ventuzelo is a senior security researcher and the CEO & founder of FuzzingLabs. After working for the French Ministry of Defense, he specialized in fuzzing, vulnerability research, and reverse engineering. Over the years, Patrick has created multiple fuzzers, found hundreds of bugs, and published research on topics such as Rust, Go, Blockchain, WebAssembly, and Browser security. At FuzzingLabs, he leads the team developing FuzzForge, an open-source platform that applies AI and workflow automation to vulnerability research. Patrick is also a regular speaker and trainer at international security conferences, including BlackHat USA, OffensiveCon, REcon, RingZer0, PoC, ToorCon, hack.lu, and NorthSec.
